# Interest Calculator - User Manual

Welcome to the Interest Calculator WebApp! This guide will help you manage your finances effortlessly.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Registration**: Create an account with your Name and Email to start tracking your money.
- **Login**: Securely access your dashboard from any device.

## 2. Dashboard Overview
- **Available Amount**: This is the total sum of money you have lent out (to Borrowers) or the net balance of your transactions.
- **Payments Due**: A slider showing upcoming or active payments that need attention.
- **Role Switching**: Use the "Refresh" icon in the top right to switch between **Borrower Panel** (Blue) and **Investor Panel** (Orange).

## 3. Managing Contacts
- Click **"Add Transactions"** and then select **"New Customer"** to create a profile.
- You can categorize people as Borrowers (those you lend to) or Investors (those you borrow from).

## 4. Recording Transactions
- Select a customer from your list.
- Enter the amount, the interest rate (%), and choose between **Simple** or **Compound** interest.
- The system will automatically update the total balance for that customer.

## 5. Interest Calculator Utility
- Use the calculator icon in the header to preview interest amounts before making a deal. It's a playground for math!

## 6. Theme & Accessibility
- The app automatically switches its color system based on your selected role to provide visual clarity.
